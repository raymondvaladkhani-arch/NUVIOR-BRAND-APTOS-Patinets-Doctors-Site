Understanding aging

Facial aging has four pillars.

Aging isn't one process — it's four happening at once. Understanding them explains why a single-focus treatment rarely tells the whole story.

30s40s60s

MIMIC WRINKLES

Dynamic & static expression lines

ATROPHY

Bone & soft tissue loss

◍
SKIN QUALITY

Elasticity & hydration loss

Collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid decline — skin becomes thinner and drier.

About the procedure

What is Aptos thread lift?

✓
Licensed by Health Canada

Aptos thread lift is a minimally invasive aesthetic procedure using bioabsorbable threads — small, specialized sutures designed to improve the appearance of facial contours without surgery.

The threads are gently introduced beneath the surface of the skin by a trained medical professional. Their unique multidirectional barb design is engineered to provide mechanical support, which may help improve the appearance of facial laxity in treated areas.

Originally envisioned in 1987 by Dr. Marlen Sulamanidze and refined over four generations of innovation, Aptos technology is used by trained physicians on five continents. In Canada, the Excellence Visage and Aptos Method devices are licensed by Health Canada and exclusively distributed and supported by NUVIOR.

Patient guide

Am I a good candidate?

Aptos thread lift may be appropriate for adults experiencing early to moderate changes in facial appearance who are exploring non-surgical options. A personal consultation with a certified provider is the only way to determine suitability.

  • Adults typically between 30 and 60 years of age
  • Thin to medium skin thickness with mild to moderate laxity
  • Seeking a refresh that retains your facial identity, not a transformation
  • In generally good health with realistic expectations
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding
  • No active skin infections in the treatment area

This list is for general guidance only. Your certified Aptos provider will conduct a thorough assessment during consultation.

Setting the record straight

Common myths about thread lifts.

There's a lot of misinformation online about thread lifts. Here's what trained Aptos professionals want patients to know.

MYTH

"Thread lifts look artificial or overdone."

FACT

With proper technique and patient selection, Aptos threads are designed to support natural facial repositioning and contour. Results aim to look refreshed, not different.

MYTH

"Thread lifting permanently damages tissue."

FACT

Aptos threads are bioabsorbable and gradually break down while supporting the body's natural collagen response. Future aesthetic options remain available.

MYTH

"All thread lifts are the same."

FACT

Thread technologies differ significantly in material composition, design, longevity, and clinical support. Speak with a certified provider to understand which option may be right for you.

Transparency

What to expect — honestly.

Aptos thread lift is a well-established procedure, but it's still a medical intervention. Here's what trained providers want you to know before your consultation.

Common temporary effects

  • Mild swelling at treatment sites
  • Tenderness or pressure sensation
  • Slight bruising in some patients
  • A feeling of tightness in treated areas
  • Possible slight asymmetry in the first few days

These effects typically resolve within a few days to two weeks. Your certified provider will review what is normal during your consultation.

When to contact your provider

  • Increasing redness or warmth at treatment area
  • Persistent or worsening pain after the first week
  • Any signs of infection (fever, discharge)
  • Thread visibility or skin contouring
  • Any concern that doesn't feel right to you

Your certified Aptos provider will give you complete post-procedure instructions and contact information. Reach out — they would rather hear from you than not.
